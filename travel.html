Getting to Madison

Flying

Madison is served by the Dane County Regional Airport. Depending on where you are flying from, there may be more affordable options to Milwaukee or Chicago, both of which have bus services which will take you to Madison.

By Bus

There are several long-distance bus services which travel to Madison. They all have a pick-up/drop-off point downtown at the UW-Madison Campus

  • Badger Bus (from Milwaukee)
  • Van Galder Bus (from Chicago)
  • MegaBus (from Chicago or Minneapolis)

Getting around Madison

If you do not drive or decide not to rent a car, there are many ways to get around Madison.

City Bus

Madison's city bus system will take you most places in a short trip. Fares are $2 one-way (cash only). You can use Google Maps to search routes or visit the Madison Metro website.

By Bike

Madison is a very bike-friendly city. If you don't bring your own, there is a short-trip bike rental service. More info on their website: BCycle Website.

Cab Services

  • Union Cab Cooperative: 608-242-2000
  • Badger Cab: 608-256-5566
  • Green Cab: 608-255-1234

Ride Sharing Services

Both Lyft and Uber operate in Madison.
